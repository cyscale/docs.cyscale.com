Billing & Usage

If you would like to get started or update your current plan, contact us at sales@cyscale.com.

Plans​

  • All plans include the following:
  • Unlimited connectors
  • All supported providers (AWS, Azure, Google Cloud, Alibaba Cloud, Kubernetes, Okta, Google Workspace, Microsoft Entra ID)
  • Asset inventory
  • Out of the box Controls (400+)
  • Alerts
  • Integrations (Teams, Slack, Webhook)
  • Jira integration
  • Email notifications (alerts and weekly digest)
  • Regulatory frameworks (ISO 27001, SOC 2, PCI-DSS, HIPAA, and more)
  • Out-of-the-box policies
  • Custom policies
  • PDF/CSV reports
  • and a lot more

Pro​

  • Automatic + manual, unlimited syncs
  • Unlimited users
  • Up to 1000 assets

Scale​

  • Up to 5000 assets

Enterprise​

If your needs exceed the scale plan and/or you want to collaborate more closely, email us at sales@cyscale.com.

MSSPs​

If you provide services for your customers and want to add Cyscale to your offering, email us at sales@cyscale.com. You can read more here.

Azure Marketplace​

If you already use Azure, subscribe to Cyscale through the Azure Marketplace (or Azure Portal link). If you are not using Azure but are still using Microsoft products, you can subscribe to Cyscale through AppSource.

Invoicing​

Once we have exchanged all the required billing information, we will email you the invoices. You can pay them via wire transfer.

Usage​

The primary usage metric in Cyscale is the asset. This represents a node in the security knowledge graph. Examples of assets include EC2 instances, EBS volumes, VPCs, buckets, Azure AD users, GCP service accounts, Okta applications, etc.

Cyscale counts each unique resource seen at least once during the billing period as a separate asset.

You can always see how many assets Cyscale has read monthly on the usage page. The information could be delayed by a few minutes.

Certain assets like volume/disk snapshots, prefix lists, subnetworks, and other assets that are not analyzed nor used for the analysis do not count toward your usage.
